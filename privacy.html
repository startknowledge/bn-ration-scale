Privacy Policy

BN Ration Scale – Defence Ration Scale Calculator

Last updated: January 2026

At BN Ration Scale, accessible fromhttps://bnrationscale.startknowledge.in,the privacy of our visitors is one of our top priorities. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that are collected and recorded by BN Ration Scale and how we use it.

This website is designed to provide a free and informative defence ration scale calculator for Indian Army personnel, veterans, family members, and administrative users. We strongly respect the privacy of every visitor and are committed to protecting it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us through our Contact page.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

BN Ration Scale is built with privacy-first principles. We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information.

  • No name collection
  • No email address collection
  • No phone number storage
  • No Aadhaar, PAN, or service number collection
  • No login or registration requirement

All calculator inputs such as rank, service years, pay values, and entitlement parameters are entered voluntarily by the user and are processed entirely within the browser.

2. How Calculations Work

All calculations performed on BN Ration Scale are executed locally in the user’s web browser using client-side scripts (JavaScript). None of the entered data is transmitted to our servers.

This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No calculator input is saved
  • No history of calculations is retained
  • No server-side database is involved

Once you close or refresh the page, all entered data is automatically cleared.

3. Cookies and Browser Storage

BN Ration Scale may use limited cookies or browser storage mechanisms to enhance user experience.

Cookies may be used for:

  • Remembering basic site preferences
  • Improving page loading performance
  • Anonymous analytics tracking

These cookies do not store personal data and do not identify you as an individual.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Doing so may slightly affect site functionality but will not prevent access to core tools.

4. Analytics and Traffic Monitoring

To understand how users interact with our website, we may use standard analytics tools such as Google Analytics.

Analytics data may include:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device type and browser
  • Approximate geographic region (non-precise)

This data is anonymous and aggregated. It cannot be used to identify individual users.

The purpose of analytics is to:

  • Improve website usability
  • Fix errors and performance issues
  • Understand content relevance

5. Advertising and Google AdSense

BN Ration Scale may display advertisements through third-party advertising networks such as Google AdSense.

These ads help support:

  • Website hosting costs
  • Maintenance and updates
  • Development of new tools

Advertising partners may use cookies or web beacons to show relevant ads. This process is governed by their respective privacy policies.

BN Ration Scale has no control over third-party cookies used by advertisers. Users are encouraged to review Google’s advertising privacy policy for more details.

6. Data Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we still take website security seriously.

Measures include:

  • Secure hosting environment
  • HTTPS encryption
  • Regular monitoring for vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Users access the site at their own discretion.

7. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as government references or informational resources.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. Users should review the privacy policies of those websites separately.

8. Children’s Information

BN Ration Scale does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has provided information through this website, please contact us immediately so appropriate action can be taken.

9. Disclaimer

This website is an independent informational platform. It does not officially represent the Indian Armed Forces or the Ministry of Defence.

All results generated by the calculator are estimations intended for planning and educational purposes only.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or site functionality.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
