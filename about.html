About BN Ration Scale – Defence Ration Scale Calculator

Your Comprehensive Defence Ration Calculator (India)

Welcome to BN Ration Scale, a free and user-friendly online calculator designed specifically for members, veterans, and families of the Indian Defence Forces who want easy, accurate estimates of ration scales and entitlement quantities.

This tool has been crafted with care to offer clarity and practicality, helping defence personnel and their loved ones better understand how ration quantities are estimated under government-approved norms.

While many calculators exist, BN Ration Scale goes beyond simple forms — we focus on transparency, correctness, and usability for those who deserve precise planning tools.

Our Mission — Empower Defence Personnel with Useful Tools

At BN Ration Scale, our mission is straightforward:

To build intelligent, accurate, and fully free online tools that help Indian defence personnel, veterans, and their families obtain fair estimates for their daily needs and entitlement calculations.

Ration scales are an important part of logistics and planning for personnel and their families. Whether you’re calculating ration scales for:

  • Deployment planning
  • Unit logistics forecasting
  • Family provisioning
  • Administrative requisition estimates

BN Ration Scale helps you convert complex ration rules into simple, understandable numbers — instantly and without cost.

Who Is This Tool For?

1. Serving Indian Army Personnel

Whether you’re a:

  • Sepoy (Sep)
  • Lance Naik (LNk)
  • Naik (Nk)
  • Havildar (Hav)
  • Junior Commissioned Officer (JCO)
  • Officer

You can use this tool to estimate your ration scales, quantities, and projected supplies according to regulated military norms.

2. Veterans and Ex-Servicemen

After retirement, many veterans revisit ration entitlement values for documentation, pension planning, or family provisioning. This tool gives an easy way for veterans to estimate and understand their data at a glance.

3. Family Members and Dependents

Many family members manage logistics planning or support daily ration planning for defence personnel households. This calculator gives them a trusted benchmark.

4. Logistics Officers and Unit Administrators

Officers and clerks dealing with daily supplies can use this tool to cross-verify manual calculations, or as a quick reference.

Why This Tool Matters

In official documents, ration scale norms and entitlements are buried inside references and government manuals. These resources are authentic but not always easy to read or interpret, particularly for people without administrative training.

BN Ration Scale bridges that gap by transforming:

  • Government tables
  • Calculated logic
  • Supply norms

Into a simple online form that instantly calculates your personalised ration requirements.

With this tool, you will never need to juggle charts or cross-references — we do the heavy lifting so you can focus on planning and logistics.
