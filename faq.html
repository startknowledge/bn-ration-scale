Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is BN Ration Scale?

BN Ration Scale is an informational platform providing ration-related calculation tools for educational understanding.

Is this an official government website?

No. BN Ration Scale is not affiliated with any government authority.

Is the website free?

Yes. All tools are completely free to use.

How accurate are the calculators?

Calculators are built using standard logic, but results should always be verified with official sources.

Do you store user data?

No. We do not store personal or calculation data.

Can results be used for official work?

No. The website is for educational purposes only.

Is the site mobile-friendly?

Yes. The website works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

If you have feedback or suggestions, please reach out via our Contact Page

Will new tools be added?

Yes. We regularly improve and expand the website.
