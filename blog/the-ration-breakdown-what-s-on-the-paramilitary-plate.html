  9. Plan for special dietary needs: Ration scales should be planned to accommodate special dietary needs, such as food allergies or intolerances.
  10. Continuously monitor and evaluate ration scales: Ration scales should be continuously monitored and evaluated to ensure they meet the needs of paramilitary personnel and are aligned with the latest nutritional research and guidelines.
