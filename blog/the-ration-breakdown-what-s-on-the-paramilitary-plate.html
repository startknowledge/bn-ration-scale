Introduction to Paramilitary Rations

Paramilitary rations are designed to provide the necessary nutrients for personnel to perform their duties in challenging environments. The typical paramilitary ration consists of a combination of **non-perishable** and **perishable** items, including meats, fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. The ration scale is carefully planned to ensure that personnel receive a balanced diet, with the right mix of **macronutrients** and **micronutrients**. Components of a Paramilitary Meal

A typical paramilitary meal consists of the following components:

  • Protein sources: Meat, poultry, fish, eggs, and legumes
  • Whole grains: Rice, bread, pasta, and cereals
  • Fruits and vegetables: Fresh, canned, or dried fruits and vegetables
  • Dairy products: Milk, cheese, and yogurt
  • Snacks: Nuts, seeds, and dried fruits
