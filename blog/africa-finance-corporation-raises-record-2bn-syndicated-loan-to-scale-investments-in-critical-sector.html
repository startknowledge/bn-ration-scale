Introduction to Africa Finance Corporation

The Africa Finance Corporation is a development finance institution established in 2007 to provide financing solutions to support the development of infrastructure, energy, and other critical sectors in Africa. The corporation has a strong track record of investing in these sectors, with a portfolio of over $6 billion in assets under management. The AFC's investment strategy is focused on supporting the development of sustainable and inclusive infrastructure, energy, and other critical sectors that are essential for economic growth and development.
