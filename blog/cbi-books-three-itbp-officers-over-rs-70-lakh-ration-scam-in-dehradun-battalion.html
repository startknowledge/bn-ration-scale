CBI Books Three ITBP Officers Over Rs 70 Lakh Ration Scam in Dehradun Battalion: Understanding the Issue and Preventing Future Scams

The Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) has recently booked three Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP) officers over a ration scam worth Rs 70 lakh in the Dehradun battalion. Introduction to the Ration Scam
