  • Implement a system of e-procurement: The Indian military can implement a system of e-procurement to ensure transparency and accountability in the procurement process.

  • Establish a central monitoring system: The Indian military can establish a central monitoring system to track ration supplies and detect instances of corruption and mismanagement.

  • Take disciplinary action against corrupt personnel: The Indian military should take disciplinary action against personnel found guilty of corruption and mismanagement to deter others from engaging in similar activities.
